Curriculet Data Project

Curriculet is an e-reading platform that allows K-12 English teachers to assign full length books, news articles and web media. It tracks student progress and assesses student competancy across a variety of ELA standards.

Understanding and using data to make classroom decisions is crucial to the lives of teachers. Conveying this information in a way that is helpful to English teachers is critical to the success of the product.

Research

Because teacher time is extremely limited, understanding where Curriculet can enable the teacher to most efficiently utilize data is crucial.

I spent many sessions embedded in the classroom understanding how teaching is evolving in a digital world.

Initial Designs and Iteration

The cycle of the classroom involves two phases: assigning work and assessing the student. Assessment can happen on multiple levels, ranging from immediate to long term.

English teachers have varying and often surprising comfort levels with numerical data. Re-framing the data in the context of classroom language helps with this.
