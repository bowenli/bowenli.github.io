Introduction

I worked on Hearsay Social's CRM team which focused on leveraging social media to create rich customer relationship management data, including things like life updates through Facebook, family photos, and general life information.

Our users needed to be able to quickly pull up relevant information about who they were going to contact.

Research

Our users wanted to interact with their clients in a more meaningful way that isn't done today. They wanted to be able to bring up salient points about their clients' lives and social media was the best way to do this. Today, professional advisors work on a time-based system where they contact their clients on a fixed schedule.

What if your financial advisor was able to see when you want to buy a house or have a child? They would be able to contact you at an appropriate time intead of sending you a postcard every 6 months. This is the core of social CRM.

Working closely with users, I determined the relevant pieces of information that they cared about when contacting clients. I also iterated with them to fine tune the UX for their workflow.
