Introduction

I worked on Hearsay Social's CRM team which focused on leveraging social media to create rich customer relationship management data. This meant using Facebook's data to help personal advisors connect with their clients.

Personal advisors needed to be able to quickly pull up relevant information about who they were going to contact.

Research

What if your financial advisor was able to see when you want to buy a house or have a child? They would be able to contact you at an appropriate time intead of sending you a postcard every 6 months. This is the core of social CRM.

I talked to financial advisors and independent insurance offices to understand their motivations for contacting their clients. This involves not only an individual, but workflows between primary advisors and assistants.

The design revolves around client management. Because an advisor will go through dozens to hundreds of contacts in a single day, she wants all the relevant information to be available immediately. She wants to be able to remember past conversations and reconnect the relationship quickly.
